what is a hype ?

"hype" , or Hyper-local Ephemeral Thread is a collection of posts that is tagged to a location. Like a college or a local tea-shop or the nearby mall.

This collection of posts is created when people in that area choose to share , discuss or ask for information.

You as a user can post anything only to a hype in your surroundings but can view hypes in anyplace , anywhere in the world
